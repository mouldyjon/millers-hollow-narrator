# Miller's Hollow Narrator - Voice Narration Script

This document contains all the narration text for voice recording with ElevenLabs.

**Voice Suggestions:**
- Male voice, deep and mysterious
- Slow pace with dramatic pauses
- Slightly creepy/atmospheric tone
- British accent preferred

**Recording Settings:**
- Format: MP3, 128kbps
- Clarity: High
- Stability: Medium-High (for consistency)

---

## Day Phase

### `discussion-begins.mp3`
"The village gathers in the square. Accusations fly. Who is the werewolf among you?"

### `voting-time.mp3`
"The time has come to vote. On the count of three, point at the player you wish to eliminate. The player with the most votes will be eliminated. One... Two... Three... Vote now!"

**Recording Notes:**
- Pause 1-2 seconds between each number (One... *pause* Two... *pause* Three...)
- Emphasize "Vote now!" for the action cue
- Keep the countdown pace steady and dramatic

### `elimination.mp3`
"The village has spoken. Step forward and reveal your role."

### `no-elimination.mp3`
"The votes are tied. No one is eliminated today. Night will come again."

**Recording Tips:**
- Record in a quiet environment
- Use a consistent tone throughout
- Add natural pauses (1-2 seconds between sentences)
- For dramatic effect: slow down key phrases
- Record multiple takes and choose the best
